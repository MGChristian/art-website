PAINTINGS

SALVADOR MUNDI

ARTIST NAME: Leonardo Da Vinci

DATE:1449-1510

MATERIAL USED: : Oil on walnut panel

DESCRIPTION: The painting is the only Leonardo work in private hands and was authenticated very

Like his earlier works, Leonardo used the sfumato technique to create softened lines around Jesus face

and robes. The lines of his hands that appear closest to the viewer are sharpest

THE CREATION OF ADAM

ARTIST NAME: Michael Angelo

DATE:1508-1512

MATERIAL USED: : Paint and Plaster

DESCRIPTION: depicts the passage from the Book of Genesis which describes God creating Adam and giving life to the first man.

THE LAST SUPPER

ATIST NAME: Leonardo Da Vinci

DATE: 1495-1498

MATERIAL USED: : Oil paint and Tempera on Plaster

DESCRIPTION: Jesus foretold his sacrifice through the symbols of bread and wine,

making amends for Adam and Eve's original sin

The Storm on the Sea of Galilee

ARTIST NAME: Rembrandt

DATE:1633

MATERIAL USED: : Oil PAINT

DESCRIPTION: Christ and his followers are embroiled in a struggle to gain

control of their boat during an intense storm.

The Last Judgment

ATIST NAME: Michael Angelo

DATE: 1535-1541

MATERIAL USED: : Paint, Plaster

DESCRIPTION: all will stand to be judged by God at the Second Coming of Jesus Christ. In early Christian art the scene

is one of Christ as judge, the resurrection of the dead, the weighing of souls, the separation of

of the saved and the damned, and representations of heaven and hell

Starry Nights

ARTIST NAME: Vincent Van Gogh

DATE: June 1889–June 1889

MATERIAL USED: : Oil PAINT

DESCRIPTION: The curving, swirling lines of hills, mountains, and sky, the

brilliantly contrasting blues and yellows, the large, flame-like cypress trees

, and the thickly layered brushstrokes
