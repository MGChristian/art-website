ARCHITECTURE

BURJ KHALIFA

ARTIST NAME: Adrian Smith, George J. Efstathiou, Marshall Strabala

DATE: 2004

LOCATION: Dubai, United Arab Emirates

DESCRIPTION: The strength of its design stems not only from its awe-inspiring verticality but also from its sleek silhouette. Wrapped in a glass curtain wall with steel mullions that catch the Arabian sun, the building tapers gradually from its Y-shaped base, with setbacks culminating in a 700-foot spire.

HARPA CONCERT HALL

ARTIST NAME: Henning Larsen Architects and Batteríið Architects

DATE: 2011

LOCATION: ReykjavÍk, Iceland

DESCRIPTION: The crystalline shell, conceived by artist Olafur Eliasson, wonderfully complements the structure’s aggregate of jagged, geometric volumes. At night, exterior LED strips activate, transforming the waterfront landmark into a shimmering beacon of beauty.

LA SAGRADA FAMíLIA

ARTIST NAME: Francisco de Paula del Villar and Antoni Gaudi

DATE: 1982

LOCATION: Barcelona, Spain

DESCRIPTION: The Basílica i Temple Expiatori de la Sagrada Família, shortened as the Sagrada Família, is an unfinished church in the Eixample district of Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. It is the largest unfinished Catholic church in the world.

PETRONAS TOWERS

ARTIST NAME: Cesar Pelli

DATE: 2011

LOCATION: Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

DESCRIPTION: Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia DESCRIPTION: The elegant modern design of the Petronas Twin Towers has made the building famous around the world. The outline of the structure resembles an immense ‘M’, symbolising Malaysia and the grandeur of the country’s aspirations and ambitions.

LEANING TOWER OF PISA

ARTIST NAME: Bonanno Pisano

DATE: 1173 and continued over 200 years of wars

LOCATION:Pisa, Italy

DESCRIPTION: one of the truly iconic works of medieval European architecture. Its distinctive lean makes it instantly recognisable; while the 207 columns spaced around its eight stories are make it an astonishing example of Romanesque architecture.

TAJ MAHAL

ARTIST NAME: Bonanno Pisano

DATE: +1631 to 1653

LOCATION: Agra, India

An immense mausoleum of white marble, built in Agra between 1631 and 1648 by order of the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his favourite wife, the Taj Mahal is the jewel of Muslim art in India and one of the universally admired masterpieces of the world's heritage.
